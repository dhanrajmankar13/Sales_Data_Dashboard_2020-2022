# Sales_Data_Dashboard_2020-2022

📊✨ Power BI Project Showcase: Sales Dashboard (2020–2022)

Excited to share my latest **Sales Dashboard**, where I analyzed 3 years of business data to uncover meaningful insights and trends 🚀

💡 **Key Metrics at a Glance:**
🔹 💰 Total Sales: 99M
🔹 💸 Total Cost: 76M
🔹 📦 Total Quantity: 13K
🔹 🧾 Transactions: 5,095
🔹 📈 Total Profit: 23M

📊 **Insights from the Dashboard:**
✔️ Monitors, Mouse & CPU are the top-selling categories
✔️ Strong sales distribution across multiple states in India 🌍
✔️ Leading brands like Intel, Samsung, and Dell dominate market share
✔️ Balanced contribution from multiple product categories

👥 **Team Performance:**
Featuring contributions from supervisors driving operational success and sales growth 💼

🎯 **Key Takeaways:**
🔸 High demand for essential hardware components
🔸 Diverse brand competition shaping the market
🔸 Data-driven insights enabling smarter decisions
🔸 Opportunity to optimize low-performing categories

This project highlights how **data visualization + storytelling** can turn complex datasets into powerful business decisions 📈
